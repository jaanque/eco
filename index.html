Biography

Pau Lladó is a jazz musician known for his soulful improvisations and captivating performances. With a career spanning over two decades, Pau has graced stages worldwide, collaborating with renowned artists and releasing critically acclaimed albums. His music blends traditional jazz roots with contemporary influences, creating a unique and timeless sound.
